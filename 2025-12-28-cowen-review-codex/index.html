Books (and a Few Essential Reads) He Endorsed

Endorsements only: clear praise plus a stated reason. This list covers the books he singled out as best, seminal, or enduring.

Best Book

Robert Tombs - The English and Their History

Clear winner; compelling enough that readers asked for more like it.
"Clear winner... everyone should read it... Utterly compelling."
Best Of Year

Ashlee Vance - Elon Musk

Flagged as one of the best books of the year and more important with time.
"All the more important, right?"
Top Biography

Charles Moore - Margaret Thatcher: At Her Zenith

One of the best biographies of the last decade.
"One of the best biographies of the last 10 years... unbelievable."
Seminal

Garett Jones - The Hive Mind

Enduring impact; widely cited and still influential.
"Held up very well... seminal work."
Deep Research

Scott Sumner - The Midas Paradox

A thorough, long-gestation argument about deflation in the Great Depression.
"A very thorough book... worked on for something like 20 years."
Strong Pick

Richard Thaler - Misbehaving

A needed, solid book about economists and economic ideas.
"There should be more books about economics... that was a good one."
Modern Classic

Michel Houellebecq - Submission

Called the most classic fiction of the last 20 years; reread-worthy and philosophical.
"Rewards rereads... incredible."
Classic Status

Kamel Daoud - The Meursault Investigation

A retelling of Camus that has already become a classic.
"The retelling of Camus has become a classic book."
Standout Fiction

The Widower - Singapore novel

Stuck with him as a genuinely great book.
"The Widower is a great book."
